template<typename MotionModelType , typename ObservationModelType >
void cuberl::estimation::KalmanFilter< MotionModelType, ObservationModelType >::update ( const input_type &  input)

Updates the gain matrix , the state vector and covariance matrix P using the given measurement z_k according to the following equations.

K_k = \hat{P}_{k} * H_{k}^T * (H_k * \hat{P}_{k} * H_{k}^T + R_k )^{-1} x_k = \hat{x}_{k} + K_k * (z_k - H * \hat{x}_{k} P_k = (I - K_k * H_k) * \hat{P}_{k}
